The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR where the communication relates to pre-contractual or contractual matters, and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR for general business communication. Where statutory retention obligations apply, the legal basis is Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR.

Business correspondence is retained only as long as necessary for the respective purpose and, where applicable, in line with statutory commercial and tax retention periods.

5. Cookies, local storage, and consent

This website uses a consent banner for optional analytics. Your decision is stored in your browser's local storage under the key mfs-cookie-consent. This storage is used only to remember whether you accepted or declined optional analytics, so the banner does not need to be shown again on every visit.

If you decline, Google Analytics is not loaded by this website. If you accept, Google Analytics may be loaded as described below. The consent choice is stored as a technically necessary preference. Any non-essential storage or access connected with analytics takes place only with your consent under the TDDDG. The legal basis for analytics processing based on that consent is Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR.

You can also withdraw consent at any time by clearing this website's local storage in your browser settings.
